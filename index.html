About Me

I am lucky enough to call myself a San Diego native. Got my BA at University California, San Diego in Political Science. Decided late in the game that politics weren't for me. Went on to get a MA in International Relations while studying abroad in five different countries.

Ended up not doing much with either of my degrees. I joined the Peace Corps and served as an English Teacher in Ukraine, and now I currently work at REI. You might find me climbing rocks or sewing quilts. And now, coding.
